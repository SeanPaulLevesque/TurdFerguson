COLORS
Speaking metaphorically, an eloquent orator's tongue is this color
Walnut, cinnamon &amp; chocolate are 3 tasty shades of this
To ebonize is to stain something this color in imitation of ebony
Carnations &amp; sweet Williams belong to this "colorful" family of flowering plants
In general, Miss Manners says, shoes of this color should be worn only between Memorial Day &amp; Labor Day
