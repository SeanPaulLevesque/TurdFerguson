FAIRY TALES
She asked Hansel &amp; Gretel, "Nibble, nibble, gnaw, who is nibbling at my little house?"
French poet Charles Perrault, not a big bird, was the first to publish her tales
Some say eggs, honey, butter, wine &amp; snuff were in the basket she carried to granny's house
In one tale by the Brothers Grimm, she had a sister named Rose-Red &amp; they both married princes
The miller's daughter promised to give Rumpelstiltskin this if she became queen
