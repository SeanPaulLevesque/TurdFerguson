STRAITS
In 1990 the Soviet Union &amp; the U.S. urged the building of an international park along this strait
Sailing ships rounding South America once avoided this strait because of heavy storms &amp; fog
At the time Alexander the Great led his troops across this strait, it was known as the Hellespont
The first known swim across this strait bounded by  the English Channel &amp; N. Sea was by Matthew Webb in 1875
This strategic strait is bounded by Iran on the north &amp; east &amp; the Arabian Peninsula on the south
