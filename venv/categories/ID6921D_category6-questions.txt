(<a href="http://www.j-archive.com/media/2021-01-26_DJ_05.jpg" target="_blank">Jeff Probst presents the clue.</a>) Though cold &amp; tired when she reached the "Carpathia" from a Titanic lifeboat, <a href="http://www.j-archive.com/media/2021-01-26_DJ_05a.jpg" target="_blank">she</a> realized that some women had lost everything, so she rallied first-class passengers &amp; raised $10,000 before the "Carpathia" reached New York; she was unsinkable indeed

(<a href="http://www.j-archive.com/media/2021-01-26_DJ_24.jpg" target="_blank">Jeff Probst presents the clue.</a>) <a href="http://www.j-archive.com/media/2021-01-26_DJ_24a.jpg" target="_blank">This</a> 1936 Olympian enlisted when World War II broke out; in 1943, his plane crashed into the ocean, &amp; he survived on a raft for 47 days, only to be taken as a prisoner of war where despite more than two years of captivity &amp; torture, he remained unbroken, also the title of a bestseller about him
