WOMEN IN MUSIC
This "Girl On Fire" performer chose her last name in reference to the piano &amp; because "it can open so many doors"
"I Will Survive" indeed; in 2020, 40 years after her hit, this singer was back on top, winning a Grammy for Best Roots Gospel Album
In 2015 Rolling Stone named her &amp; Ike No. 2 on their list of the Greatest Duos of All Time
With more than 100 million albums sold, this "Man! I Feel Like A Woman!" singer is among the top-selling country artists of all time
This frontwoman for the Go-Go's has also had solo hits like "Mad About You" &amp; "I Get Weak"
