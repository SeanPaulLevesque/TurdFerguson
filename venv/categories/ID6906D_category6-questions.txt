ENTERTAINING BROTHERS
These brothers starred in the classic film "A Night at the Opera"
This band's jam-oriented southern rock sound produced tunes like "Ramblin' Man" &amp; "Whipping Post"
Last name of <a href="http://www.j-archive.com/media/2021-01-15_DJ_17.jpg" target="_blank">actors</a> Gustaf, Bill &amp; Alexander, sons of another well-known actor
Singers Bill Medley &amp; Bobby Hatfield called their duo this after the reaction they got from African-American audiences
Around 1895 these brothers devised an early motion picture camera &amp; projector they called the Cinematographe
