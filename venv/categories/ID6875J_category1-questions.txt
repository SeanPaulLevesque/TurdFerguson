ARMED FORCES
East Indian word for "dust-colored", it's a U.S. Army &amp; Air Force uniform material
In the Roman army it was the rank of a man who commanded about 100 soldiers
The Enterprise, Kitty Hawk, Nimitz, Midway, &amp; Forrestal are the 5 classes of these
In 1941, they enlisted in film as "Buck Privates" &amp; "Came Home" in the 1947 sequel
U. S. military pilots who fly higher than 50 miles up earn this pilot ranking
