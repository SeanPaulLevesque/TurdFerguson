ON THE COVER
The July 1969 Esquire cover asked, "what words should the first man" here "utter that will ring through the ages?"
The plane flown by this alliterative man is seen <a href="http://www.j-archive.com/media/2020-10-22_DJ_13.jpg" target="_blank">here</a> on the August 29, 1908 <a href="http://www.j-archive.com/media/2020-10-22_DJ_13a.jpg" target="_blank">cover</a> of "Scientific American"
The December 6, 1924 Saturday Evening Post cover featured a Christmas scene by him
In February 1963 Car &amp; Driver featured this classic Triumph sports car &amp; the same-named fighter plane
The July 1963 National Geographic cover listed a Carl Sandburg article on these 2 "burgs" subtitled "Just a Hundred Years Ago"
