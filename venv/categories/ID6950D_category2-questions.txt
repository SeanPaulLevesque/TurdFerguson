PAINTINGS
Cassius Coolidge did a whole series of paintings of "Dogs Playing" this card game, some commissioned to advertise cigars
The Musee d'Orsay has this 19th century artist's "Dance at the Moulin Rouge"
Shortly before a more famous painting, he did "Woman with a Pearl Necklace"
Though perhaps better known for his sea subjects, in 1872, he <a href="https://www.j-archive.com/media/2021-02-24_DJ_11.jpg" target="_blank">painted</a> a group of boys playing Snap the Whip
He depicted the horrors of war &amp; the execution of his fellow Spaniards by Napoleon's troops in "The 3rd of May 1808"
